About


The Cryptocurrency Club at Pitt is a student-run, undergraduate organization composed of students interested in cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology.


We provide blockchain education through presentations, workshops and guest speakers and serve as a platform to discuss every aspect of cryptocurrencies from the technology to the economics to the business applications.
